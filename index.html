Personality Disorders


Image from Behavioral Health Florida

Personality disorders is an complex mental disorder in terms of both identifying and controlling it. Personality disorders are a group of mental illnesses that involves long-term patterns of thoughts and behaviors which are unhealthy and inflexible. The causes of personality disorders is mostly a blend of genetics and environmental factors. Your genes may make it more likely that you develop a personality disorder, and what happens to you in life may set a personality disorder into motion. Research suggests that genetics, abuse, and other factors contribute to the development of obsessive-compulsive, narcissistic, or other personality disorders. People who have a dysfunctional home life in early childhood and adolescence can develop personality disorders in later life.
